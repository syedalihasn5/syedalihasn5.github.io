Etisalat FTTH – Complex E2E Scenarios

End-to-end validation for telecom order flows (activation, deactivation, cease, TOSS). Coordinated across systems and data layers to ensure service quality.

E2ETelecomSeleniumRobot FrameworkPythonTestRail

Overview

We implemented scenario-based test design across cross-functional teams. Key checks covered order orchestration, provisioning, and customer‑facing milestones with reliable rollback/cleanup routines.

Highlights

  • Risk‑based prioritization for critical order paths.
  • Reusable fixtures & data setup to simulate provisioning states.
  • Structured bug triage with stakeholders for quick resolution.

Results

  • Improved confidence for FTTH rollouts across regions.
  • Reduced manual verification through automated orchestration checks.
